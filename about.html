THE VISION

Apiary solutions is committed to promoting and developing connections between thriving, ambitious high school students and college students who attend the worlds most elite universities. By leveraging a diverse, cross-generational network of college and high school students, Apiary carries out this mission through intense personal mentorship, with the aim to successfully guide students through the college application process and beyond.
